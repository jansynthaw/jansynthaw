The Impact of NYSERDA's Solarize Campaigns on the Cost of Going Solar

The purpose of this map is to identify the impact of group purchasing models for residential solar-energy systems in New York State. Group purchasing campaigns—as well as streamlined solar permitting—may decrease the cost to consumer to install solar technology and may aid in the entry of solar into underserved, lower income communities. The map will also help identify areas of opportunity for new Solarize Campaigns. For more information about group solar purchasing campaigns, please visit NYSERDA's website .

Layers

  • Average Price Per Watt ($/Watt) for residential installs (by county, for the year 2015)
  • Solarize Campaign Locations (by city or county)
  • Average $/Watt in Solarize Campaign Locations
  • Cities where PACE financing and unified solar permitting has been adopted
  • Census block groups where over 51% of households are Low or Moderate Income (LMI)
